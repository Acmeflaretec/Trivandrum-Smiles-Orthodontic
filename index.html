About Us

Trivandrum Smiles Orthodontic And Pediatric Multispecialty Dental Care

Who We Are:

Trivandrum Smiles Multispecialty Dental Care is a leading dental clinic nestled in the heart of Thiruvananthapuram, Kerala. Established by passionate professionals, Dr.Abhinandh Krishna MDS and Dr.Revathy M Nair MDS our clinic is a testament to our commitment to providing exceptional dental care.

Our Mission:

At Trivandrum Smiles, our mission is to redefine dental healthcare by offering comprehensive and personalized services. We strive to create a warm and welcoming environment, ensuring that each patient feels comfortable and confident in their journey towards a healthy and beautiful smile.
